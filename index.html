Our story

Made by feel, the way my mother taught me.

I'm Perriann — a small-batch potter, cold process soap maker, and baker in San Diego. I learned to make things during weekend baking sessions with my mom, who never measured a thing.

"She taught me to pour until she said stop, to scoop without measuring — to trust my gut."

That's how everything here gets made. Because each piece is shaped, poured, and cured by hand, no two are ever quite alike — and that's the point.
